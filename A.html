About Us.

Welcome to a world where color and emotion intertwine, where each brushstroke whispers a story, and every canvas is a doorway into imagination. I'm Ishita, the heart and hands behind Iquara Collections, a place where art transcends the ordinary and invites you into the extraordinary. My art is born from a deep connection to the world around usâ€”the fleeting light of a s unset, the dance of shadows on a quiet street, the emotions we carry but seldom express. Each painting is a journey, not just for me as the creator, but for you, the viewer. I work with acrylic, watercolor to explore the delicate balance between chaos and calm, between boldness and subtlety, letting my intuition guide the brush as much as the vision in my mind. This space is not just an exhibition of my work; it's a sanctuary for those who seek to feel more deeply, to see beyond the surface, and to connect with art in a personal way. Whether you're drawn to the intricate details of nature or the abstract echoes of human experience, my paintings invite you to pause, reflect, and perhaps, discover a part of yourself within them.

Thank you for stepping into my artistic world. Here, every piece has its own voice, and I hope one will speak directly to yours.
